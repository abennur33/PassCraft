How to generate a Gmail App Password

  1. Go to your Google Account settings page: https://myaccount.google.com/
  2. Click on "Security" in the left menu.
  3. Scroll down to "Signing in to Google" and click "2-Step Verification."
  4. Set up 2-Step Verification if not done already.
  5. Scroll down the menu and click "App passwords."
  6. Choose "Mail" and your device type (or "Other" if not listed).
  7. Click "Generate" to get the app password.
