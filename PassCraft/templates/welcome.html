About the App


Welcome to PassCraft! This versatile application allows you to generate custom cards and e-tickets for various events and purposes. Whether you need personalized membership cards or digital e-tickets, this app has got you covered.


You can customize each card with individual names, and even include QR codes for ticket verification. It's a powerful tool for event organizers and businesses seeking to streamline their identification and ticketing process.

Before you begin:

  1. Have a template ready to customize for ticket/card generation.
  2. Have a spreadsheet ready with each recipient's name and email (if you are planning on sending the cards over email).
  3. Ensure the email you will be sending the cards through has a valid app password to use. A tutorial is available later in the process.
